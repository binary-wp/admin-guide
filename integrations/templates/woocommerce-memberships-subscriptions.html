Memberships & Subscriptions

This guide covers the combined setup of WooCommerce Memberships and WooCommerce Subscriptions — the recommended configuration for managing paid memberships with recurring billing. Both require WooCommerce as a base.

How It Works Together

A Subscription Product handles the payment — it charges the member on a recurring basis (monthly, annually, etc.). A Membership Plan controls what the member gets access to — restricted content, member-only pages, discounts.

When a customer purchases a subscription product that is linked to a membership plan, the membership is automatically created. The membership lifecycle follows the subscription:

  • Active subscription → active membership
  • Subscription renewed → membership extended
  • Subscription cancelled/expired → membership paused or ended

Some subscription products are linked to a membership plan (core membership tiers). Others are standalone add-ons (e.g., additional services or donations) that don't grant membership access.
