WooCommerce Memberships

WooCommerce Memberships controls access to restricted content and manages member accounts. It requires WooCommerce as a base.

Settings: {{woo_settings_link}} → Memberships tab

How It Works

A Membership Plan defines what members get access to — restricted pages, products, discounts, and member-only content. Membership is granted when a customer purchases a product linked to a plan.

Members can manage their account via the Members Area on the My Account page, where they see their membership details, restricted content, and available discounts.

Related Plugins

  • WooCommerce Subscriptions (recommended) — when combined, memberships are automatically tied to subscription lifecycle: active subscription = active membership, cancelled subscription = paused/ended membership
