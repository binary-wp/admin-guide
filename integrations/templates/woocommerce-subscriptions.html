WooCommerce Subscriptions

WooCommerce Subscriptions adds recurring payment support to WooCommerce — subscription products that charge customers on a regular schedule. It requires WooCommerce as a base.

Settings: {{woo_settings_link}} → Subscriptions tab

Product Types

Subscriptions introduces two product types:

  • Simple Subscription — a single recurring product (e.g., annual donation, add-on service)
  • Variable Subscription — a subscription with variations/tiers (e.g., membership levels with different pricing)

Subscription Lifecycle

Each subscription has a status (active, on-hold, cancelled, expired) managed automatically by payment events. Subscribers can manage their subscriptions from the My Account page.

Related Plugins

  • WooCommerce (required) — base e-commerce platform
  • WooCommerce Memberships (recommended) — when combined, purchasing a subscription product can automatically grant membership access. The membership lifecycle follows the subscription: renewal keeps it active, cancellation ends it.
